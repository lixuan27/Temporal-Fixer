What the frozen editor gives us — and what the fixer must repair.

Why the middle panel is the whole method. It costs nothing but clean video and one pass of the frozen editor: no instruction, no synthesized edit pair, no VLM filter. Any editor can produce it, so the fixer trained on it inherits editor-agnostic behaviour.

Watch before you read. Both an over-smoothed output and a reverted edit score better on flicker than a correct fix. Every checkpoint in this project is judged on video first, table second — that rule is what caught the reversion failure.
