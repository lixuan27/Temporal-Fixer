Architectural

Channel-concat conditioning [noisy state | draft | source] with zero-initialized new channels, so step 0 is exactly the pretrained video model. Rectified flow whose σ=1 endpoint is re-anchored from Gaussian noise to the draft — the path is short, so one Euler step suffices (measured: K=1 ≡ K=4).

Methodological

A training-pair engine that needs no edits: clean video + its own per-frame identity reconstruction by the same frozen editor. Plus source-free conditioning to close the "copy the source" shortcut, and a residual anchor to the draft so the fixer may change time but not appearance.

Strategic — the actual contribution

Co-adaptation of the two stages. Diagnose and stabilize the frozen editor's stochastic protocol first, then re-calibrate the fixer's training distribution to the resulting true draft statistics. This is the step that broke a ceiling five loss/scale interventions could not.

Claim: this is a new, independently learnable task — not post-processing, not filtering — with an editor-agnostic, zero-paired-data recipe. 06
