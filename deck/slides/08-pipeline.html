Stage A — the editor is frozen and outside the recurrence

Per-frame image editing, 4-step distilled, guidance is a no-op in this pipeline (no negative prompt), the seed policy is the only effective stochasticity dial. No temporal context ⇒ it cannot drift, but it flickers. At training time it produces an identity reconstruction of clean video; at test time it applies the actual edit.

2.2 s / frame @720p4-step distilled, full precision (H200)
37 s / frame40-step non-distilled — 17× slower, sharpness 636 vs 542
Structure-exactevery car, sign and pole stays in place — the precondition for the whole method

Shared latent space — verified not to be the bottleneck

33 pixel frames → 9 latent frames (block size 3), 16-channel Wan-VAE, prompt encoded once per clip with umT5. We measured the codec's own floor before trusting any latent-space result.

1.08VAE round-trip flicker on clean video
1.05natural-video floor — the codec adds essentially nothing
10+draft flicker ⇒ ~9 units of genuine headroom for a latent-space fixer

Stage B — the only trained component

Conditioning is channel-concatenated into the patch embedding: 16 → 48 channels = [noisy state | draft latent | source latent], new channels zero-initialized so training starts exactly at the pretrained model. The objective is edit-anchored rectified flow (slide 9). Under source-free training the source slot is filled with the draft itself.

1.3Bparams — 14B was tried and gave no genuine gain
K = 1single Euler step at inference (K=1 ≡ K=4, measured)
37 GBpeak VRAM per GPU at 33 frames, batch 4, bf16

Runtime — chunk-by-chunk, constant cost

Block-causal attention with a fixed-size rolling KV cache; positions are re-indexed so they never leave the range the model was trained on ⇒ arbitrary length, training-free. Nothing is precomputed; output is written while the clip is still arriving.

O(1)memory and latency per chunk, at any stream length
1 stepthe fixer's marginal cost — the editor dominates the budget
No edit statedrift cannot accumulate through the edit path

Instruments — all frozen, all off-the-shelf, all re-run by us

RAFT (flow, always computed on the source) · DINOv2 (style-invariant structure) · CLIP (edit preservation + on-promptness) · VBench temporal dimensions · a VLM judge for holistic quality. Competitor checkpoints are executed on our videos with our protocol.
