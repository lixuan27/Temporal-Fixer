Second benchmark (colour + material edits, 40 clips, never seen in training): flicker 39.13 → 12.59 (−68%), smoothness +0.103, subject consistency +0.137, holistic 2.89 → 3.25, edit preservation 0.951. The recipe transfers across editor, edit type and benchmark.

Established

  • Single step = four steps (−56.9% vs −56.1%).
  • Beats naive flow smoothing on flicker and edit preservation simultaneously.
  • The latent codec is not the bottleneck (round-trip 1.08 vs floor 1.05).
  • Zero paired edit data throughout.

Falsified — by us

  • Structure loss, perceptual loss, 2× duration, flow-guided temporal loss, 10× capacity: all five slide along the same frontier.
  • The 14B "capacity gain" was reproduced exactly by a zero-training pixel blend of the 1.3B output.
  • Our own "motion-faithfulness" advantage was flicker masquerading as motion. Claim retracted.
