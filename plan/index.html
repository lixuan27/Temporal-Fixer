01

The thesis in one line

A per-frame image editor gives a flickery draft — no temporal context, so it can't drift. A causal video model, trained on clean video only, fixes the temporal consistency. No paired edit data.

  • Goal — streaming V2V editing for in-vehicle pass-through AR: edit a driving stream in real time, chunk by chunk.
  • Why a fixer, not an editor — generating consistent video is hard and drifts; fixing a given draft is an easier, bounded task.
  • Why it trains for free — supervision is clean video; the "edit" lives entirely in the per-frame image model at inference.

05

How the fixer is trained — reconstruction, no pair synthesis

Training data = clean video + its degraded self

draft = per-frame identity im2im of a clean clip (mimics editing flicker). target = the clean clip. No (input, prompt, edit) triplets to synthesize.

Train it not to edit — only to de-flicker

Multiple domains & styles in one degradation bank ⇒ the model learns the general de-flicker operator, robust across editors — never a specific edit. (GaussFusion §4 artifact simulation.)

  • Objective — Edit-Anchored flow: anchor on the draft, flow to the clean target, single-step, causal (Self-Forcing init).
  • Supervised on clean video — the model recovers the clean appearance of the draft, so it de-flickers and sharpens.
06

The decisive fix — source-free conditioning (don't revert the edit)

With target = source and the clean source in the conditioning, the fixer learns to copy the source — at test it reverts the edit. Watched, not from metrics: a daytime edit snapped back to night.

Sourced (before)

de-flickers ✓  but reverts the edit ✗ — the low flicker is the reversion.

Source-free (fix)

feed z_source := z_draft — no source to copy ⇒ keeps the edit appearance and de-flickers ✓.

Confirmed on driving edits: the daytime / pop-art look is preserved after fixing, no longer reverted to the night source.

09

Staged plan

Stage A

Editor

Qwen-Edit-2509 int4 + Lightning-v2, 4-step (nunchaku), structure-EXACT. Highest-quality distilled editor; calibrate flicker to the deployment magnitude.

Stage B

Fixer

Wan2.1-1.3B causal DiT, source-free reconstruction, multi-style degradation bank, distribution-matching objective to stay sharp. Streaming via Infinity-RoPE.

Stage C

Eval

Watch first. Flow-warped flicker · VBench temporal · CLIP edit-preservation. Targets: OpenVE-Bench SA · V2V-Bench · VBench-temporal on driving.

  • Open — align the reconstruction prompt distribution to the deployment editor; add driving training data; scale the fixer base if quality demands.
